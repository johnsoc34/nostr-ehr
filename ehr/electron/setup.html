Demo Mode

Explore NostrEHR with a temporary keypair on a public relay. Perfect for evaluating the software before setting up your own infrastructure.

⚠️ Demo Mode Notice
A temporary keypair will be generated and you'll be auto-logged in. Data is published to a public relay. Do not enter real patient information.
What you'll be able to do:
• Browse the full EHR interface and all clinical modules
• Create test patients and encounters
• Try PDF generation, growth charts, immunization tracking
• See how Nostr-based encrypted records work
When you're ready to set up for real, click ⚙️ Configure Practice in the demo banner.
